How It Works

Our RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system combines traditional retrieval methods with advanced language models:

  1. Upload: Add documents to create your personalized knowledge base
  2. Index: Our system processes and indexes your documents
  3. Retrieve: When you ask a question, relevant information is fetched from your documents
  4. Generate: The system creates comprehensive answers based on the retrieved information
